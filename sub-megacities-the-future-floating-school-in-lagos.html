Floating School in Lagos

 
click here

The area of Makoko in Lagos is known as a 'water community' because it is built entirely over water. Transport is by boat and all of daily life is done on the water. Children in Makoko learn to swim before they walk on land - water is a way of life.!

But there are problems - regular flooding, rising sea levels from climate change and bad drainage. One idea is to develop 'floating buildings' and the first one to be built is a floating school. This new school building can house 100 children and teachers.!

These buildings are shaped as triangles and sit on top of a pontoon that floats on the water. This is one way of dealing with lack of good housing and the problems of climate change all in one. Imagine going to a floating school!
