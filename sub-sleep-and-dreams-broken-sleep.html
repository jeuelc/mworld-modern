Broken sleep

Unfortunately, although sleep is natural process controlled by our bodies, and is essential to our survival, there are a lot of people who have trouble sleeping or have a sleep disorder.

There are two main types of sleep disorders: parasomnias and dyssomnias. A lot of sleep disorders are not harmful to us, but some can affect how we behave during the day, how well we learn and even our health.

But you dont need to have a sleep disorder to disturb your sleep. Simply playing on your smartphone or going to bed at different times each night can push your body clock out of sync and make you feel tired, moody and hungry!

Parasomnias

A parasomnia is any sleep problem connected to waking or partial waking and/or moving from one sleep state to another. Like sleepwalking!

dyssomnias

A dyssomnia is any sleep problem that causes difficulty going to sleep, staying asleep or too much daytime sleepiness.
