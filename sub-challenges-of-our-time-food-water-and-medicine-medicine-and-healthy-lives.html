You know of course how important it is to the quality of your life to keep yourself healthy. When world leaders set the Millennium Development Goals for a better future, three of the eight goals were related to health. These goals related to the health of babies and young children and their mothers, and to the prevention of diseases such as malaria and HIV/AIDS.

There are many reasons why mothers and young children suffer from poor health. Lack of resources such as food, clean water, medicine, and medical care is a key reason, as is lack of education.

Medicine and medical care can be expensive; but they can also be quite cheap. Many of the best advances in medicine and health in poor communities are basic vaccination, education, access to basic medicines, access to trained nurses and so on.
