Of course, TV networks can't make us do things that we don't want to we come together to watch talent shows and the like because we enjoy doing it.

Reality TV is a 'glue' that can hold family and friends together through a shared interest. We value the time we spend having fun with people we care about, either by watching the show or talking about it later.

Even if you don't like these shows very much, you might take an interest in them because this is what your friends are talking about at school.

For many people, watching and discussing reality television has become a social ritual; it's something we do to connect to others and participate in our communities.
