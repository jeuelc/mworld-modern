Why Do People go on Reality TV?

How would your life change if you suddenly became famous?

Many of us wonder why people want to go on reality television.

The answer on talent shows is clear: people want the chance to do what they love perform.

But this can come at a price. When Susan Boyle became an overnight YouTube success after appearing on Britains Got Talent, she soon discovered that she wasn't ready for fame, especially because audiences were more interested in her personal life than her brilliant voice.

All reality contestants must be ready for this intrusion. It's possible that some people want to be on reality TV because they think you are only important in society if you appear in the media.

Everyone wants approval and reality TV can be a powerful way of getting recognition. For some, just being noticed brings acceptance.

Would you want to appear on reality TV? Why?
