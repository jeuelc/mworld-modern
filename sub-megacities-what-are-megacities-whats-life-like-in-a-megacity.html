What's life like in a megacity?

Life in a megacity can be tough sometimes. There might not be a proper house to live in, only a shack. Many millions of people in megacities earn very little money, and what they do earn often comes from finding something to make or sell from their own resources. This is called the 'informal economy', where people don't have regular jobs and instead create their own ways to earn the money they need to live.

Even kids sometimes have to earn money, often instead of going to school, and they do that by finding something to sell, or delivering messages, or picking over a rubbish dump. In some places there are more people who own a mobile phone than have access to a proper toilet!

Life for poor people is made even more difficult when they are threatened with having their homes destroyed because someone else wants to build on the land.
