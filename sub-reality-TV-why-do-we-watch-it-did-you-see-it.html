Did You See It?

 
click here click here

Events like the Olympics, elections or religious ceremonies can bring a large number of people together at the same time, whether they choose to watch it live or sit together while watching on television.

But now, televisions aren't just in the living room. You can watch television on your iPad, in your bedroom, on the train or bus, even in the bathroom! So why do we still come together to watch important television events live?

You could record the final of your favourite show and watch it later, by yourself, but would you really want to? Could you resist first finding out the result on Twitter? Wouldn't you miss gossiping with your friends about what happened as it happened?

The success that reality TV has in bringing people together to watch the final episodes, eviction nights or special events tells us that we still enjoy coming together at the same place and time to share experiences.
