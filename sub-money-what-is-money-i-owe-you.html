I Owe You!

What happens if you want to buy something really expensive like a car or a house, but you don't have the money up front? Sometimes, you can borrow from the bank. Money is a convenient way to maintain accounts (that is, record how much you owe) and lets you defer payments (pay them back later in small amounts), making sure that lenders and borrowers do not lose.

Another example of how money works in this way is the receipt you get when you deposit money in the bank. This is a promise to you by the bank that they will give you the value of your deposits whenever you want it, just like an IOU!
