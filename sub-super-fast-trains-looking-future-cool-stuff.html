Cool Stuff Costs A Lot!

Super-fast trains are cool, and with the possible advances like MagLev and vacuum tubes they could get even cooler!

But sometimes being cool technology isn't enough for it to become a usable technology. Advanced technology can often mean expensive technology, and this is the case for super-fast trains.

For companies or governments to provide super-fast trains for people to ride in, they have to make money, or governments have to use money they receive from the citizens through taxes. So either train tickets have to cover the costs of building and operating the trains, or tax money has to be spent on trains instead of other equally important things a government does, including other transport options like roads and normal rail, as well as schools, hospitals and that kind of stuff...

Ideally, with the right balance, everyone can be happy, and hopefully super-fast trains can be whizzing you around now or in the future for a long time!
