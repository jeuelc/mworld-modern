An interesting story and look that catches the eye can go hand in hand, and they are both very important parts of marketing.

To become a celebrity, and to remain one, you will need to learn to sell yourself to the media.

David Beckham is a great example of this. He is a talented footballer, but even in his early career he was valuable to teams because he was so well known. Real Madrid (a famous Spanish football team) sold Beckham replica kits while he was playing for the club and was able to make a big profit. It's no accident that the world's most famous player only played for the world's most famous clubs; by working together their combined marketing was even more successful.

Finding new ways to show your image to the world is also important. We call this 'marketing durability' and it can be the difference between a one-hit wonder and a long-lasting career. Think about David Beckham's career. Is it the same as the career of someone like Corey Delaney? How are they different?
