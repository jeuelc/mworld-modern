You know that you need to drink water to survive. You could just go down to a river and drink there. But what if the river is polluted? You would get very sick.

So you need to capture the water and treat it first. That means getting rid of the bugs and pollution in the water that would make you sick. Then you need to transport it to where people live and add pipes and taps so people can use it lots of work to do!

From 1990 to 2010, more than two billion people gained access to clean water, but there are many people who still dont have access to clean water.

There are also 2.5 billion people who don't have access to improved sanitation. That means they dont do poo in a toilet they have to find somewhere else to do it!

We need water for drinking, sanitation, farming, transport, bathing, gardens and more... water really is the source of life!
