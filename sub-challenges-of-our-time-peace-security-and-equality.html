Peace, Security and Equality

The world is, mostly, a beautiful, amazing place, and people have created some amazing art, music, movies, theatre, scientific discoveries and more. But people sometimes create problems and very unpleasant things too, such as war, violence and discrimination.

Water and food are basic necessities for life, but so are peace and security. War might be fun in a computer game, but it's not fun in real life!

If you just listened to the media, it might seem like the world is full of war and conflict. But in reality most of the world is more secure than at any other time in human history.

Equality has also improved in many societies over the past 100 years. But there are still many places where people are punished, or have fewer rights than other people, because of the way they look, or what they believe, or because they are female.
