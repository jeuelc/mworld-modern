Making It Better?

You know how important peace, security and equality are for people to live happy and healthy lives. So how do we get better at this? Can we eliminate war and conflict completely? Can we provide equality for all people?

It would be mighty hard to get rid of war, conflict and discrimination completely. But there are lots of things we can do to make it better. As far as possible, we can get rid of the most dangerous weapons, such as chemical weapons and nuclear weapons. We can improve the levels of understanding and co-operation between people and countries, to reduce the chances of conflict.

We can use the internet and social media to make people in all countries aware of cases of discrimination, and to promote equality.

But most of all we can encourage young people to learn more about the amazing world we live in and to treat everybody in it with respect.
