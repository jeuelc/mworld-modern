Electronic Banking - How Safe Is It?

Most money is made up of electronic entries, which show up as deposits in a bank statement. Electronic money can't be damaged by floods, or perish, and in some ways this makes it the safest way to store wealth. But could it still be stolen?

Paper notes and coins (currency) might occasionally be stolen by a bunch of bank robbers who physically go into the bank and take it but thefts from banks can also occur electronically. Banks spend a lot of money to protect your deposit money from 'hackers' through special computer security and coding.
