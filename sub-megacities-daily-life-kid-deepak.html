A Day in the Life of a Megacity Kid


Deepak from Mumbai

Hi, I'm Deepak. I'm eight years old and I live in Mumbai. My parents died when I was younger which means I'm an orphan and I don't really have anyone to look after me. Each night I sleep on the dirt floor of the shacks of other people.

When the sun comes up in the morning I get up quickly and go out onto the

muddy streets looking for rubbish I can recycle for a few coins.

There is nowhere to wash and I have to go to the toilet in

the street or in the canal. Sometimes I have to dive into

the dirty canal to find bits of rubbish.

If I make enough money I can buy some fruit from the

market, or some chappatis. I like to play football

with my friends. The thing I would most like

to do is go to school but it is hard here

because there aren't many schools

and I have to earn money to eat.
