Reality TV

Did you know that many British people think that the judge of UK's X Factor, Simon Cowell, would make a good Prime Minister? Or that the Australian Prime Minister and opposition leader thought that Big Brother was so important that they BOTH appeared in the house before the 2013 election?

Reality TV is something that most of us see or hear about every single day. It entertains us, makes people famous and can even change how we think about big issues like politics or nutrition.

But what is reality TV? Why are we so interested in it? And why is it so good at making people famous?
