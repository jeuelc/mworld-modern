Run On The Bank

 
click here click here

A 'run on the bank' happens when a lot of a bank's customers try to withdraw their deposit money at the same time! The bank ends up short of reserves and is forced to sell off things it owns at cheap prices to come up with the necessary cash.

In the worst case, this could cause a bank to go into bankruptcy and out of business.

If lots of people lost faith in the banking system, this would lead to a banking crisis and a run on the banking system as a whole. This could lead to a big reduction in the supply of money and in the end, economic growth would be reduced.
