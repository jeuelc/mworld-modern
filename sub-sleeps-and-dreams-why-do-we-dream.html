Why do we dream?

How and why we dream is still a mystery, but there are lots of ideas about it.

Some people think our dreams sort out all the stuff we've learnt during the day and keep some of it to store into memory, and get rid of the rest.

Other people think dreams help us work out our emotions. If something is bothering you or you are excited about something during the day, you might dream about it at night. But the dream might be a tiger chasing you through the jungle rather than what you are actually worried about! Or you might find that you can fly rather than being excited about something in your dream.

But some people think dreams are just random thoughts caused by our brains being so active during REM sleep. In truth, no-one really knows!
