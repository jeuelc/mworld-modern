Challenges of Our Time

Have you ever wondered what it will be like in the year 2040? You'll be an adult then, right? Probably with your own children, lots of friends, and hopefully a good job and a nice home.

But what will the world be like in 2040? Will it look like the world today? Will cars, trains and planes look the same? Will farmlands look the same?

In today's world there are many people who don't have enough food, water or medicine. Cars, planes and power stations burn fuels that damage the Earth's atmosphere. There are wars, famines and natural disasters. There are people punished because of the way they look, or what they believe, or just because they are a girl or a woman.

So hopefully the world will look a bit different in 2040. The world will never be perfect, but hopefully it will still be beautiful and hopefully everybody will have a good job and nice home!
