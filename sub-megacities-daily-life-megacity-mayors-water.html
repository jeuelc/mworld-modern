Water is essential to everybody's life. In megacities, managing water can be a headache! There are so many people in megacities, and they grow so quickly, sometimes not everybody has access to clean drinking water.

Millions of people in megacities live in homes that have no running water. There is also the problem of water pollution. Many people build their small shacks near water because they need it, or because it is the only spare land. But that means the water gets polluted. People might have to go to the toilet in the water, because there are no toilets. And there is a lot of rubbish, which pollutes the water as well.

The other problem with water is that there is sometimes too much of it! Many fast-growing megacities are in parts of the world that have tropical storms and heavy rains. When it rains, the streets flood and millions of people can be swamped with dirty, polluted water. Managing water is one of the main problems in megacities.
