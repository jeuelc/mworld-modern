Whatever they do, all celebrities need to have a life story that you care about.

Have you heard of Jade Goody? She appeared on UK Big Brother when she was only 19. At first, people in the media made fun of her. But as Jade continued to appear on more and more reality programs we learned about her life, her difficult upbringing and her struggle to be a good mother and a better person.

When Jade passed away in 2009, her story was so well known that the Prime Minister of the UK saluted her courage.

We might never know what the 'real' Jade was like, but her compelling life story transformed her into a national figure.
