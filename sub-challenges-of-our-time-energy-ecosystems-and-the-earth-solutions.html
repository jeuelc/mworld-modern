So what are the solutions to environmental issues such as climate change and degraded ecosystems? There is no perfect solution at this stage. If there was, people would have implemented it already.

New technologies can help. If you can develop a way to make solar panels more cheaply, or a way of storing electricity more efficiently for car batteries, you might make it easier to use renewable sources of energy rather than fossil fuels.

Economics and politics can help too. If a local community has ownership of a forest or fishery, they would have more incentive to protect it for the long-term. If an outsider owns it, they might have more incentive to use all the resources while they can.

Most global issues have a complex set of causes: economic, political, cultural, environmental, scientific, technological, and so on. If you want to help solve a global challenge, your solution will surely need to consider each of these factors!
