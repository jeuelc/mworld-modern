How do you solve global challenges? Theyre hard issues to understand. They require lots of creativity and hard work to solve. Too much for one person to try by themselves, right? Yes, that's right you will need a team!

Connecting to other young people will help you to understand the issues better and will give you the energy, inspiration and resources to tackle global challenges.
