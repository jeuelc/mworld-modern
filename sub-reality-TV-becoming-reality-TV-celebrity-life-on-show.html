One thing that is definitely real is the effect of celebrity. Because celebrities are selling a complete image, they have to endure a lot of intrusion into their private lives. If you are not prepared for this before you become a celebrity it can be very upsetting.

Lots of people who become celebrities when they are children have to grow up onscreen. They are scrutinised for their every move. When Hannah Montana's Miley Cyrus appeared on the 2013 MTV Music awards, some people criticised her for being a bad role model. Would you like to carry responsibilities like this?

So being a celebrity can give people that you have never met access to details of your day-to-day life. Would you want to become a celebrity, knowing these consequences?
