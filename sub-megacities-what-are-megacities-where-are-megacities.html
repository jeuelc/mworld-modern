Where are Megacities?

 
click here click here

Megacities exist in many different countries around the world, but they are all very different. There are megacities in the USA such as New York and Los Angeles and in Europe, such as Paris (France) and Moscow (Russia).

But most megacities, and especially the ones growing the fastest, are in poorer parts of the world. The megacities of Africa such as Lagos and Cairo are very different in the way they grow and the livelihoods of their people than cities such as Istanbul in Turkey, Shanghai in China, or Sao Paulo in Brazil.

China has many megacities including Shanghai, Beijing, and Guangzhou, as well as the huge urban area of Congqing. The megacities of Latin America and Asia are growing very fast; sometimes whole new neighbourhoods are built in just a few days! The megacities of Europe and the USA grow more slowly.

How many megacities do you know of around the world?
