War... On The Decline?

War and conflict have been part of human history since the earliest times. But why do people go to war? Sometimes it's to get access to resources such as water, gold, oil or land for colonisation. Sometimes it's to get control of lucrative trading routes.

Sometimes people go to war because of fear and rivalry, or because of the ego of their leaders. There are many different causes of war, and often many of them go together.

The good news is that, according to academics such as Michael Eisner from the United Kingdom and Steven Pinker from the United States, the rates of war and violent crime are much lower today than they were in other times of human history. But there are still many violent conflicts in the world today, especially civil wars. And modern weapons are so dangerous that the human cost of war today can be very high indeed.
