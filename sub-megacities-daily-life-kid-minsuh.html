A Day in the Life of a Megacity Kid


Minsuh from Seoul

click here click here

Hi, I'm Minsuh. I'm 12 years old and I live in Seoul with my parents in a tiny apartment on the 14th floor of a high-rise tower. I have an amazing view from my bedroom window. I can see out over the city and the Han River.

Every morning at 7am, I have to get ready to go to school

because it is a long way by bus across the crowded city. I learn

science, mathematics and English but my favourites are ceramics

and dance. After school I go to a 'hagwon', to do extra classes.

I get home very late, sometimes at 10pm and then I like

to practise my piano and do some homework. It is a long

day and I get very tired. But I want to be a biologist

      when I grow up and to do that means working hard to

get into university.

My favourite day is Sunday when mother takes

me to the market to buy delicious 'bindaetteok',

crispy pancakes with meat and vegetables.
