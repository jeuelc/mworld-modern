What Are They?

Super-fast trains help get people from one place to another fast! They travel at speeds of at least 250 km/h and some can travel as fast as 500 km/h. Thats as fast as some planes! No wonder theyre also known as bullet trains in Japan.

Super-fast trains are used all around the world: throughout Europe, China and Japan, and soon to be in America. They are most useful to move a large number of people between big cities that are a long way apart.

Most super-fast trains travel on the same rails as normal trains, but a small number float above rails using magnets!
