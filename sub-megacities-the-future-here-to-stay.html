Megacities are here to stay.

Across the world, more people live in cities now than live outside of cities, and that has never happened before. It means that we live in an 'age of cities', when cities are the main places where people live, work, eat, and sleep.

Experts say there are going to be more and more megacities across the world as more and more people move to the already very big cities to find work. It is likely that the world's population will be 70% urbanised. And some experts say that megacities will join together to form 'mega-regions' - urban areas of more than 100 million people!

All of this means that cities are more and more important to how our world works, and that is especially true for megacities.
