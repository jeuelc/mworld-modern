Daily Life

Daily life in a megacity involves getting around and making a living in an enormous place! How would you travel around such a big city?
For the poor living in slums and squatter settlements, life is challenging. Daily life might include travelling long distances to find work for the day, or carrying water for washing and cooking. It might include getting up very early to sell produce at a market, or hunt around a rubbish tip to find things to be resold or recycled. There might not be enough water to wash and very often there might be rubbish building up right outside where you sleep.Daily life would involve trying to survive all of these challenges.
