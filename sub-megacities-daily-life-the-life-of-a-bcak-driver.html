The Life of a Becak driver

 
click here click here

Basir is a becak driver in the megacity of Jakarta, Indonesia. He has lived with his family in Jakarta for 10 years since they moved from a small village in central Java because he could not find work.

Basir drives his becak, or bike taxi, from very early in the morning, sometimes until late at night. Mostly he likes driving because he gets to meet lots of new people and he loves to chat and hear about where they are going. Recently he met some people visiting from Australia and took them to all the best places to visit in Jakarta. It was tiring, though, pulling a family of four along all day!

Sometimes he takes children of his neighbourhood to school in his becak. There's so much traffic in Jakarta it is quite hard to move about, but Basir tries to take only the small lanes and the backstreets to avoid the trucks and cars. Basir tries to have one day off each week to rest his tired legs and oil the wheels of his becak.
