Camels Vs Computers

In a traditional bartering system, it would be hard to know what the thing you had to trade was worth. For example, if a camel was worth 1000 apples and 10,000 apples were worth 5000 oranges and 20,000 oranges were worth two computers, then how many computers would a camel be worth? Whaaaat?! In the modern world with all the stuff we have,trying to measure value this
way would be crazy, if not impossible! Money lets you understand in a simple way what one thing is worth in relation to another. In other words, it provides a common way to measure value, or a 'common unit of account'. For example, if a camel is worth $700 and a computer is worth $2,100, then you could easily work out that a computer is worth three camels!
