What Can You Do?

So you've learned about some of the challenges of our time. It might sound a bit daunting, but there's still plenty of reason for optimism. Many communities and countries around the world have made major progress over recent decades. The world remains a beautiful place. Human beings remain capable of amazing inventions and creativity.

So what can you do? Young people have unlimited imagination and energy. Tap into your own talents and resources and maybe one day you can help to solve some of these thorny global challenges.

We suggest three ways to give yourself the best possible chance to make a difference: learn more about the issues; connect with other talented young people; and innovate!
