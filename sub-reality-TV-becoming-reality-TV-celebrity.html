We know that reality TV creates celebrities, but have you ever wondered how?

Lots of people complain about the amount of attention the media devote to celebrities who aren't talented, clever, or even very interesting.

But talented or not, all celebrities are good at one thing: catching your attention. They do this by recognising the ingredients of fame that don't have much to do with special abilities.

Celebrities stay celebrities as long as audiences want to know more about them as people. Strangely, this often doesn't have much to do with either audiences or celebrities; it's more to do with how the media offer us new and engaging ways to connect with the famous. But if viewers choose not to watch a show, do you think it will keep being aired?

So, what does a celebrity need to have, apart from talent? What sort of celebrity would you like to be?
