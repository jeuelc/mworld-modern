What does innovate mean? Innovate means to develop new ideas and new ways of doing things. If you develop a new way of doing your homework faster, and still get the same marks from your teacher, that is innovation!

The brothers who developed the first airplane, the scientist who first discovered X-rays, the company who developed the first mobile phone these people were all innovators who helped to solve problems of their time.

If you can innovate you might be able to develop new ways of producing clean energy cheaply, or you might develop a new vaccine for a disease like malaria. Or you might become an economist and develop a new way to distribute food around the world.

All you will need is creative thinking, a talented team, some hard work and a bit of luck...!
