The Future

There are going to be more megacities in the future and they're going to get even bigger. But there are such big challenges and problems, it means people are going to have to start thinking creatively!

Imagine you're in charge. How can you make sure everyone has somewhere safe to sleep and clean water to drink? How can you make sure every child can go to school? What about providing enough trains and trams and buses for everybody to get around to work, to school and to have fun?

These are some of the future challenges of megacities!
